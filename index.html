Who am I?

With sharpened teeth ready to try the next tasty treat, strangely large hybrid arms to reach the best souvenirs on the top shelf, tied laces to climb the tallest mountains, I'm setting out to see what the world has to show me. I'm always asked, "What Makes a Dinosaur Go on Adventures?" There's cool stuff out there, why not?

Adventures

From hiking Angel's Landing in Utah to white water rafting in Squamish, BC to seeing the latest Star Wars on opening day, I head out to find adventures on the highest peaks and within each day.
